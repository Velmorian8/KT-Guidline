Official KT Guideline

Knights Templar Moderation & Duty Handbook

Introduction

Greetings, fellow Knights. To maintain order, protect our community, and honor the spirit of the Templar Order, this Guideline Book has been created. All members, especially those with moderation powers, must follow these rules strictly and without exception. Failure to uphold these standards will result in disciplinary action.

Kwarns (Serious Offenses)

3 Kwarns = Automatic Ban

  • Spamming hateful or targeted messages
  • Inappropriate avatars (offensive/suggestive)
  • Direct threats against players
  • Harassment of clergy or players
  • Blasphemy against God
  • Bullying or ganging up on others
  • Swearing (mild or censored)
  • Discrimination (racism, sexism, etc.)
  • Disrespecting Knights Templar moderators
  • Using glitch exploits (not full hacks)
  • Bypassing chat filters
  • Advertising other groups/games
  • Spamming inappropriate memes or symbols
  • Pretending to be staff

Automatic Bans (Severe Offenses)

  • Hate speech (severe racism, bigotry, anti-religion hate)
  • Exploiting/hacking
  • Robanger activity
  • Satanist roleplay or spam disrupting the group
  • Extremely inappropriate avatars (NSFW, nudity)
  • Serious threats (death threats, doxxing)
  • Repeat harassment after warnings
  • Ban evasion (alts)
  • Raiding (mass spam, troll attacks)
  • Extreme trolling (pornographic/satanic builds)
  • Scamming players for Robux/accounts
  • Sharing harmful/NSFW links
  • Terrorism
  • Voice chat abuse (threats, sexual harassment, hate speech)
  • Direct attacks against the Catholic faith

Moderator Commands

Use strictly for moderation purposes only:

:freeze :refresh :serverban :unban :mute :unmute :kwarn :warn :warns :give :bring :to :chatlogs – View chatlogs :shutdown – Emergency shutdown

Addressing Higher Ranks

  • Grandmaster – “Your Greatness, (Username)”
  • Templar Master – “My Liege, (Username)”
  • Seneschal – “Honorable, (Username)”
  • Marshal – “Officer, (Username)”
  • Generals – “Commandant General, General (Username)”
  • Captain – “Sir/Sire, (Username)”
  • Lieutenant – “Lt. (Username)”
  • Knight – “K. (Username)”
  • Trial Knight – “TK. (Username)”

Duties of a Templar

  • Protect the Church from enemies and troublemakers.
  • Recruit new members to the Church and Templar Order.
  • Assist and maintain order during Church events.
  • Fulfill monthly quotas of service/recruitment.

Failure to meet duties without reason may result in removal. Those who serve faithfully will be honored.

Closing Statement

This guideline ensures every offense is clearly defined. Moderators should never debate what is punishable — instead, follow this handbook with fairness, discipline, and integrity. By upholding these standards, we protect our faith, our community, and the honor of the Knights Templar.

Written by Velmorian, Grandmaster of the Knights Templars
